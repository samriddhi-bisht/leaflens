Leaf It To Us- Smart Tech For Our Farmers

Snap, Scan, Succeed – Boost Your Crop Health!


Our AI enabled Image Classifier Model trained with supervised learning approach under classification algorithm, diagnosis the deficiencies of nutrients present in leaves with high accuracy and enables farmers to monitor their crop health for free!

Small-scale farmers often struggle to detect nutrient deficiencies in their crops at an early stage due to the subtle changes in leaf appearance. These nutrient imbalances can lead to poor crop health, reduced yields, and lower income for farmers.

The project addresses food security by providing real-time insights into nutrient deficiencies, leading to healthier crops and better yields. It aligns with the goal of promoting sustainable agriculture by improving small-scale farmers' access to technology.

There is a need for an affordable, accessible, and real-time solution that empowers farmers to identify nutrient deficiencies in crops based on visible symptoms in leaves, helping them take timely action to improve crop health and yield.
